Write Script In

Writing a screenplay correctly is difficult. Typically professional screenwriting software will alleviate this by helping you format things correctly. Professional software also costs money. Write Script In is a free alternative that utilizes the Fountain markdown language to beautifully craft screenplays.

Clapham School

By gutting the tables out of the old site, remodeling the structure to be based around responsive design, and throwing on a fresh coat of paint, Clapham School is now better able to position itself as a truly classical and innovative school.

Wheaton App

Based on the principal of least touches to information needed, the Wheaton App was developed to aid Wheaton College students in finding commonly desired information. Designed to adhere to both the college and iOS7 design guidelines.
